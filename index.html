Who We Are

Bridging the Gap for Every Girl

In Northern Nigeria, millions of girls are out of school due to cultural barriers, poverty, and lack of infrastructure. Educate Her is dedicated to changing this narrative.

We work directly with communities to build safe learning environments, provide scholarships, and advocate for policy changes that prioritize female education.

  • Eliminating financial barriers
  • Community sensitization
  • Teacher training & support
